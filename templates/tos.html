Overview

Fleet Safety provides automated DOT compliance document tracking for fleet owners and trucking companies. These Terms of Service govern your use of our SMS and email alert system.

SMS Consent

By onboarding as a client — whether through a verbal agreement or a written form completed with our administrators — you grant Fleet Safety permission to send you automated SMS text messages to the phone number you provide. These messages relate solely to DOT compliance, including reminders about expiring or expired documents such as medical cards, driver's licenses, and annual reviews.

Message frequency varies based on your document expiration schedule. Message and data rates may apply, based on your mobile carrier plan.

How to Opt Out

You can opt out of SMS alerts at any time by replying STOP to any text message you receive from us. Once you reply STOP, you will no longer receive automated SMS alerts.

For help, reply HELP to any message, or contact us at tryfleetsafety@gmail.com.

Your phone number and contact information are used exclusively for compliance-related communications. We do not sell, rent, or share this information with third parties for marketing purposes. See our Privacy Policy for more details.
