Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 19, 2026

What We Collect and Why

We collect phone numbers and email addresses directly from fleet owners via off-platform agreements (verbal or written onboarding). This data is entered into our private, secure portal by our administrators solely to provide automated compliance reminders. We do not share, sell, or rent your phone number or personal data to third parties for marketing purposes.

How We Use Your Information

The contact information on file is used exclusively to send automated reminders about upcoming or expired DOT compliance documents (such as medical cards, driver's licenses, and annual reviews) via SMS and/or email, and to allow our administrators to manage your account and uploaded documents.

Data Security

All client and document data is stored in a private, access-controlled administrative portal. Only authorized administrators can view or manage this information. Uploaded documents are served only to authenticated administrators.

SMS Consent & Opt-Out

By providing your phone number during onboarding, you consent to receive automated SMS alerts regarding DOT document expirations. You may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message. See our Terms of Service for full details.

Lifetime License Accounts

Accounts purchased under a Lifetime Access plan (see our Terms of Service for the full definition and sunset/termination terms) are bound by the exact same data-handling rules as standard recurring monthly accounts. Specifically:

  • We do not sell, rent, or share the phone number, email address, or other personal data of a Lifetime License holder with third parties for marketing purposes, under any circumstances or at any tier.
  • Lifetime License holders are subject to the same SMS consent, STOP/HELP opt-out mechanics, and message-frequency disclosures as all other users.
  • If Fleet Safety discontinues the service under the sunset clause in our Terms of Service, the data-handling and no-third-party-marketing commitments in this Privacy Policy continue to apply to any retained data for as long as we retain it, and we will follow our standard data deletion practices for closed accounts.
